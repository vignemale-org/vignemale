How it works

No magic. It reads your code.

Vignemale looks at your code and sets up what it needs — the database, the storage, the routes. You never describe your infrastructure twice.

Nothing is run, nothing is guessed. Your code is the single source of truth — change it, and your infrastructure follows.

In production

Prod-grade, with nothing to configure.

The behaviour a real service needs is already in the core — you don't wire it up.

  • Zero-downtime deploys
    In-flight requests finish before the old version stops. You ship a new version without dropping a single call.
  • Stays up under stress
    Slow or oversized requests are cut off automatically, so one bad call can't take the whole service down.
  • Secure by default
    Traffic to your database and between your services is encrypted and signed — and you didn't have to set any of it up.
  • Observable out of the box
    Every request is logged and traceable across services, so when something breaks you can follow it. Built in.

Serverless or Kubernetes

Pick the runtime. Keep the code.

The same app runs serverless or on Kubernetes — it's an environment choice, not a rewrite. Start cheap and bursty, move to packed nodes when traffic is steady.
